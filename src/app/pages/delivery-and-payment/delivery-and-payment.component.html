Delivery and payment

Green zone

We deliver within the green zone within 1 hour.

The minimum order amount for this zone is 300 UAH.

Yellow zone

We deliver within the green zone within 1.5 hour.

The minimum order amount for this zone is 400 UAH.

The «Monosushi» team offers an extremely convenient delivery and payment system. The clarity of our work standards ensures fast and high-quality order fulfillment for our guests.

We have divided Lviv into conditional delivery zones - green and yellow. We clearly define the delivery time for each zone.
The green delivery zone is closer to our kitchen, so orders are delivered to this zone within 1 hour. The yellow zone, which is much further away, takes up to 1.5 hours.
